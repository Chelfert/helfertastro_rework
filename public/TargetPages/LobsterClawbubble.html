The Lobster Claw and Bubble Nebula

Location in Sky

Acquisition: SkyWatcher 130-PDS. August, 2022.


Quick Facts

  • Catalog: Sharpless 157 (Lobster Claw), NGC 7635 (Bubble Nebula).
  • Discovered: 1787, William Herschel.
  • Distance from Earth: 8,000 light-years.
  • Diameter: 400 light-years.
  • Magnitude: 10
  • Best Visible: Late Summer.

    • Description
